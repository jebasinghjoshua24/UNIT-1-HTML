HTML Special Characters & Favicon Example

Here are some common special characters in HTML:
&copy; (Copyright symbol): ©
&reg; (Registered Trademark): ®
&trade; (Trademark): ™
&nbsp; (Non-breaking space): Word NextWord
&lt; (Less than): <
&gt; (Greater than): >
&quot; (Double quote): "text"
&apos; (Apostrophe): 'text'
&cent; (Cent symbol): ¢
&euro; (Euro): €
&pound; (Pound): £
&yen; (Yen): ¥
&sect; (Section Sign): §
&deg; (Degree): 32°C
&para; (Paragraph): ¶
&hearts; (Heart): ♥
&mdash; (Em Dash): —
